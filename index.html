PhD Research

Model-Free Predictive Control for Multiphase Machines

Developing a novel control scheme based on gradient analysis of electric variables. This approach removes parameter dependency, improves dynamic performance, and reduces computational burden compared to traditional MPC[cite: 58, 60].

Experimental Validation

Implementation of control strategies on a real test bed. Validation of back-EMF compensation techniques and adaptable modulation to avoid parameter usage[cite: 25, 29].
